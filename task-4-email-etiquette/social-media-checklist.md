# ✅ DO

•	Think before you post; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. 

•	Maintain a professional online presence, especially on platforms like LinkedIn. 

•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 

•	Use privacy settings to control who can view your content. 

•	Be respectful in comments and discussions, even when you disagree. 

# ❌ Don’t

•	Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly. 

•	Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content. 

•	Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment. 

•	Avoid oversharing daily activities that may compromise your safety. 

•	Do not post misleading or fake information without verification.
