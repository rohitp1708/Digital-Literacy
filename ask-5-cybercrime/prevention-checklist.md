# Part B – Prevention Checklist:
While researching online safety, I was most surprised by how easily people fall victim to UPI and financial fraud due to simple mistakes like sharing OTPs or scanning unknown QR codes. I learned that cybercriminals often use very convincing tactics, such as pretending to be bank officials or customer support agents, which makes it difficult for users to identify fraud. The availability of resources like the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and the helpline 1930 also highlighted the importance of reporting such incidents quickly to minimize losses.
As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious while making online transactions. I will always verify the source before scanning QR codes or clicking on links and will never share sensitive information like OTPs or UPI PINs. Adopting these practices will help me stay safe and avoid financial fraud.
